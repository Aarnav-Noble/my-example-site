My Favourite UFC Fighters

This page shows my 6 favourite UFC fighters, retired or not, and a description for each fighter to show why he/she was chosen. Everything mentioned in this Website is my opinion so please do not take it as a fact. ITS TIME!

Daniel Cormier

Incredible wrestler with a surprisingly good stand-up for someone as short as him. He is also a very likeable person, especially during his weekly podcasts with Ariel Helwani and commentary. He is definately one of the best wrestlers in the UFC and the best heavyweight when he was in his prime.

Max Holloway

He is by far the best boxer in the UFC with his constant pressure and unimaginable stamina. He is a very experienced fighter for someone who has not even reached his thirties yet. His improvements in each fight shows that he has not reached his prime yet which is shocking considering his achievements so far. Definately the best featherweight in all of UFC and a very likeable person.

Khabib Nurmagomedov

29-0. That's all you have to say for the Dagestani wrestler. No one has found any flaws on him and many people think he has not reached his prime yet, making his sudden retirement so sudden. He is easily the P4P in my opinion and with no one coming close as of now. His religious beliefs and discipline makes him an amazing role model for any one. Undefeated.

Israel Adesanya

The last stylebender is undefeated in the UFC and has over 100 kickboxing fights. This is shown in the fights as his incredible stand-up almost always knocks his opponents out or dominates them. His ground-game and takedown defence is getting better and better each fight which could easily make him a future P4P #1.

Darren Till

Probably the most controversial fighter from the bunch, he is one of the more underrated middleweight fighters in the middleweight division. His powerful left hand, increasingly good takedown defence and other assets makes him a fighter to look out for. He has had few setbacks but this is a new weight class and he has done well so far.

Rose Namajunas

One of the only female fighters I respect, Rose has an outstanding stand-up and an underrated submittion game. This along with her mentality and midset, before and during the fight, makes her a very smart fighter. Her along with many other female fighters in the strawweight division easily makes this best women's weightclass by far.
